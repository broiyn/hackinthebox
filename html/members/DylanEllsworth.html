Introduction

Hi, My name is Dylan! I am a third year student at UCSD studying computer science and business. In my free time I like to surf, hike, and travel. My favorite sports are football and baseball. Because my parents are from California and Minesota, I grew up watching teams from both states. Now I root for the Vikings and Chargers for football and the Padres and Twins in baseball. I travel and camp relatively often, however this last year I was limited with the pandemic. Hopefully now that people are getting vaccinated and we build up immunity, we can go back to doing the activities that we enjoy and interacting with each other in person!
